# Arimia - Beekeeping and Honey Products E-Commerce

## Project Summary

Arimia is a professional online store designed and developed on the İkas platform for a natural honey and bee products brand. A turnkey solution reflecting the brand's identity with custom CSS styles, JavaScript code, and unique header design.

## Challenge

### Business Requirements

- **Brand Identity**: Unique design conveying naturalness and trust
- **Product Presentation**: Impressive showcase of honey varieties and bee products
- **User Experience**: Easy navigation and quick purchase process
- **Mobile Compatibility**: Perfect appearance on all devices
- **Easy Management**: Panel that the customer can easily manage

### Technical Requirements

- **Platform**: Turkey-focused infrastructure with local support
- **Customization**: Custom design beyond standard themes
- **Contact**: Custom designed contact page
- **Header**: Branded, eye-catching top menu
- **Speed**: Fast loading, optimized pages

## Solution

### Platform Selection

**İkas E-Commerce Platform** was chosen because:
- Turkey-specific integrations (payment, shipping)
- User-friendly management panel
- SEO-friendly infrastructure
- 24/7 Turkish support
- Fast and reliable hosting

### Custom Developments

**Header Design:**
- Design compatible with the brand's bee-themed logo
- Category menu and search function
- Mobile responsive hamburger menu
- Cart and user icons

**Contact Page:**
- Custom form design
- Map integration
- WhatsApp direct contact button
- Social media links

**CSS Customizations:**
- Brand colors (honey gold, natural tones)
- Custom button and card styles
- Hover animations
- Typography adjustments

**JavaScript Integrations:**
- Dynamic header effects
- Scroll animations
- Form validation
- User interactions

## Features

### E-Commerce Functions
- Product catalog management
- Variant support (size, weight)
- Stock tracking
- Campaign and discount management
- Coupon system

### Payment and Shipping
- Credit card payment
- Cash on delivery option
- Bank transfer
- All shipping company integrations
- Automatic cargo tracking

### Customer Management
- Member login and registration
- Order history
- Address book
- Favorite products
- Email notifications

## Results

- Professional and trustworthy brand image
- Easy-to-manage e-commerce infrastructure
- Mobile-friendly responsive design
- Fast page loading times
- SEO optimized structure
