# Mikro ERP B2B - Sales Representative Account Integrated System

## Project Overview

A B2B sales platform fully integrated with Mikro ERP software. An enterprise solution designed with a focus on field sales representatives (sales reps), bringing together current account management, branch-based stock tracking, 3D secure payment system, and order management features on a single platform.

## Challenge

### Business Needs

Challenges faced by field sales teams:

- **Mobile Access**: Field workers' need for instant access to current accounts
- **Stock Visibility**: Real-time tracking of stock status at different branches
- **Quick Ordering**: Fast order taking during customer visits
- **Payment Convenience**: Secure online payment infrastructure
- **Account Tracking**: Detailed viewing of customer account transactions
- **ERP Integration**: Synchronization with central ERP system
- **Account Summary**: Automatic account summary sending to customers

### Technical Requirements

- **ERP Integration**: Fully compatible API with Mikro ERP
- **Security**: 3D Secure payment infrastructure
- **Multi-Branch**: Multi-branch stock management
- **Account Module**: Detailed current account operations
- **Order System**: B2B order management
- **Reporting**: Account summary and report generation
- **User Management**: Sales representative-based authorization
