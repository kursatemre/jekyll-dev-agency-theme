# How to Start an E-Commerce Site in 2025? Step-by-Step Guide

The e-commerce sector in Turkey is growing 40% annually and is expected to reach 1 trillion TL in 2025. Now is the perfect time to start online sales! In this comprehensive guide, you'll learn all the details of building a successful e-commerce site.

## Advantages of Starting an E-Commerce Site

### Why Should You Sell Online?

1. **24/7 Sales**: Your store never closes
2. **Wide Customer Base**: Reach all of Turkey
3. **Low Operating Cost**: No physical store expenses
4. **Measurable Data**: Analyze everything
5. **Fast Growth**: Unlimited scalability

### Statistics
- 60+ million internet users in Turkey
- Number of e-commerce users exceeded 45 million
- Average basket size ₺350-400
- Mobile shopping rate 65%

## E-Commerce Platform Selection

### 1. WooCommerce (WordPress)

**Advantages:**
- Completely free and open source
- Unlimited customization
- 50,000+ plugin support
- SEO friendly structure
- Compatible with all payment systems

**Disadvantages:**
- Requires technical knowledge
- Security responsibility is yours
- Hosting cost

**Ideal for:**
- Those wanting customized design
- Large product catalogs
- Blog and content-focused brands

### 2. Shopify

**Advantages:**
- Very easy setup
- Security and hosting included
- Professional themes
- 24/7 support
- Quick setup (active in 1 day)

**Disadvantages:**
- Monthly cost ($29-299)
- Limited customization
- Transaction commission
- Lack of Turkish support

**Ideal for:**
- Those wanting to start quickly
- No technical knowledge
- Small-medium businesses

### 3. Ticimax / Ideasoft

**Advantages:**
- Turkish companies, Turkish support
- All Turkish payment systems integrated
- Ready integrations with shipping companies
- Legal process assistance

**Disadvantages:**
- High monthly cost (₺500+)
- Low design flexibility
- Platform dependency

**Ideal for:**
- Turkey-focused sales
- Easy management seekers
- Support-priority brands

### 4. Custom (Custom Software)

**Advantages:**
- Completely customized for you
- Unlimited customization
- Unique user experience
- All integrations custom

**Disadvantages:**
- High initial cost (₺100K+)
- Long development time (3-6 months)
- Requires continuous maintenance

**Ideal for:**
- Corporate companies
- Special business models
- High revenue targets

## E-Commerce Site Setup Stages

### Stage 1: Preparation (1-2 Weeks)

#### Legal Procedures
- E-commerce registry certificate (2-3 days)
- KEP address (1 day)
- SSL certificate (instant)
- Distance sales contract preparation
- Privacy and KVKK texts

#### Business Plan
- Target audience analysis
- Competitor analysis
- Pricing strategy
- Marketing budget
- Supply chain planning

### Stage 2: Design and Development (3-6 Weeks)

#### Essential Pages
1. **Homepage**: Slider, featured products, categories
2. **Product Pages**: Detailed descriptions, variants, reviews
3. **Category Pages**: Filtering, sorting
4. **Cart**: Clear pricing, shipping calculation
5. **Checkout**: Secure, fast, mobile compatible
6. **My Account**: Order tracking, address management
7. **About Us, Contact, FAQ**

#### Mandatory Features
- Mobile responsive design
- Fast loading (under 2 seconds)
- Product search and filtering
- Add to cart animations
- Favorite products
- Product comparison
- Live support chat
- WhatsApp order line

### Stage 3: Payment Systems (1 Week)

#### Popular Payment Methods in Turkey

**Credit Card (70%)**
- iyzico (recommended)
- PayTR
- Param
- Sipay

**Cash on Delivery (20%)**
- Cash
- Credit card with POS

**Bank Transfer/EFT (5%)**
- Bank account information
- Automatic approval system

**Digital Wallets (5%)**
- Papara
- PayPal (international)
- Apple Pay, Google Pay

#### Commission Rates
- iyzico: 2.45% + ₺0.25
- PayTR: 2.99% + ₺0.25
- Param: 3.50%

### Stage 4: Shipping Integrations (1 Week)

#### Recommended Shipping Companies

**MNG Cargo**
- Fast delivery
- Affordable pricing
- Wide branch network

**Yurtici Cargo**
- Reliable
- Corporate solutions
- Expensive

**Aras Cargo**
- Cheap options
- Campaigns

**Surat Cargo**
- Fast

#### Shipping Costs
- 0-1 kg: ₺30-40
- 1-3 kg: ₺40-55
- 3-5 kg: ₺55-75
- **Free shipping strategy**: For ₺300+ carts

### Stage 5: Product Upload (2-4 Weeks)

#### Professional Product Page

**Mandatory Information:**
- High-quality photos (min 5-8)
- 360-degree view (recommended)
- Detailed description (min 300 words)
- Technical specifications
- Variants (size, color, model)
- Stock information
- Shipping time
- Return conditions

**For SEO:**
- Keyword research
- Title optimization
- Meta description
- Alt texts
- Product videos

#### Product Photo Tips
- White background
- High resolution (min 1200x1200)
- Different angle shots
- Usage visuals
- Size reference

### Stage 6: Testing and Optimization (1 Week)

#### Things to Test
- [ ] All payment methods
- [ ] Mobile compatibility
- [ ] Page speeds
- [ ] Cart processes
- [ ] Email notifications
- [ ] SMS notifications
- [ ] Stock management
- [ ] Return processes
- [ ] Invoice issuance

## Cost Analysis

### Initial Investment Cost

**Ready Platform (WooCommerce):**
- Hosting: ₺1,500-3,000/year
- Theme: ₺2,000-5,000
- Plugins: ₺3,000-8,000
- Setup: ₺10,000-25,000
- **Total: ₺16,500-41,000**

**Custom Design:**
- Design: ₺25,000-50,000
- Development: ₺40,000-80,000
- Integrations: ₺15,000-30,000
- **Total: ₺80,000-160,000**

### Monthly Operating Cost

- Hosting: ₺500-2,000
- Payment system commission: 2-3% of turnover
- Marketing: ₺5,000-20,000
- Customer service: ₺8,000-15,000
- **Total: ₺13,500-37,000+**

## Legal Obligations

### Mandatory Texts
- Distance sales contract
- Privacy policy
- KVKK disclosure text
- Return and exchange conditions
- Cookie policy

### E-Commerce Registry Certificate
- Obtained from Ministry of Trade
- Online application (www.eticaret.gov.tr)
- 2-3 business days
- Free

## Success Metrics

### KPIs to Track

1. **Conversion Rate**: 2-5% target
2. **Average Order Value (AOV)**: ₺300-500
3. **Cart Abandonment**: Under 70%
4. **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: ₺50-150
5. **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**: ₺800-2,000
6. **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**: 3:1 minimum
