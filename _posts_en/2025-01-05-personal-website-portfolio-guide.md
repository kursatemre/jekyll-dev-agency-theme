## Design Principles

### Minimalist and Clean

**Why?**
- Your content stands out
- Professional appearance
- Fast loading
- Long-lasting

**Key Elements:**
- Plenty of white space
- Limited color palette (2-3 colors)
- Consistent typography (2 fonts max)
- Grid-based layout
- Subtle animations

### Typography

**Font Selection:**
- **Sans-serif** (modern, clean):
  - Inter, Poppins, Montserrat, Roboto
- **Serif** (classic, elegant):
  - Playfair Display, Merriweather, Lora

**Hierarchy:**
```css
H1: 48-72px (Hero headings)
H2: 36-48px (Section headings)
H3: 24-30px (Sub-headings)
Body: 16-18px (Content)
Small: 14px (Caption, footer)
```

**Line Height:**
- Headings: 1.2-1.3
- Body text: 1.5-1.7

### Color Palette

**Brand Colors:**
- Primary: Represents your brand
- Secondary: Complementary
- Accent: For CTA buttons
- Neutrals: Gray tones

**Examples:**

**Minimal & Professional:**
```
Primary: #2C3E50 (Dark Blue-Gray)
Secondary: #ECF0F1 (Light Gray)
Accent: #3498DB (Blue)
Background: #FFFFFF
Text: #2C3E50
```

**Creative & Bold:**
```
Primary: #FF6B6B (Coral)
Secondary: #4ECDC4 (Turquoise)
Accent: #FFE66D (Yellow)
Background: #F7F7F7
Text: #292F36
```

**Dark & Modern:**
```
Primary: #1A1A2E (Dark Navy)
Secondary: #16213E (Navy)
Accent: #E94560 (Red)
Background: #0F3460
Text: #EAEAEA
```

### Responsive Design

**Mobile-First Approach:**
- 60%+ traffic on mobile
- Touch-friendly buttons (min 44x44px)
- Easy navigation
- Fast loading

**Breakpoints:**
```css
Mobile: < 768px
Tablet: 768px - 1024px
Desktop: 1024px - 1440px
Large: > 1440px
```
